Netflix Subscription & Revenue Dashboard

This Power BI project explores Netflix's subscription data, revenue trends, and user demographics through interactive dashboards, DAX modeling, and business storytelling. It includes a full stakeholder-ready presentation with business recommendations.

Project Highlights

  • Created an interactive Power BI dashboard to track Netflix subscriptions, revenue trends, and user segmentation.
  • Modeled key KPIs like Total Revenue, Top Subscription Plan, Age Group Profile, and Subscription Duration.
  • Delivered a stakeholder-ready presentation with business insights and recommendations on pricing and content investment.
  • Used Power Query and DAX for data cleaning, calculations, and dynamic filtering logic.

Tools & Techniques

Power BI • DAX • Power Query • KPI Modeling • Stakeholder Presentation • Data Storytelling

Key Business Insights

  • Basic plan is the most popular with 999 of 2500 users.
  • Most engaged users fall in the 46–51 age group.
  • US generates the highest revenue; India has potential with 12M users and lower ARPU.
  • Recommendations: regional pricing optimization, personalized content push, and real-time marketing response using data pipelines.

About This Project

This project was executed as a 24-hour end-to-end analytics sprint to simulate real-world BI delivery. It combined technical execution (ETL, dashboarding, modeling) with business communication and stakeholder storytelling.
